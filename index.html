GR Yaris

Born From WRC: The Ultimate Hot Hatch

The Toyota GR Yaris is not your typical hatchback; it’s a motorsport-inspired machine, designed with one clear purpose in mind: to dominate both the track and the road. Developed by Toyota’s Gazoo Racing (GR) division, the GR Yaris draws heavily from the world of rally racing, specifically the World Rally Championship (WRC). Unlike many other hatchbacks in its class, the GR Yaris is built from the ground up to provide a thrilling and unique driving experience, blending advanced technology with raw performance.

Under the hood, the GR Yaris features a 1.6-liter, 3-cylinder turbocharged engine, producing an impressive 268 horsepower and 370 Nm of torque. This makes it one of the most powerful three-cylinder engines ever built. Thanks to its lightweight construction and aerodynamic body design, the GR Yaris can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.5 seconds. It's not just fast in a straight line — the all-wheel-drive system ensures maximum grip, allowing drivers to tackle corners with confidence and precision.

The GR Yaris incorporates cutting-edge features like a six-speed manual transmission, GR-Four all-wheel-drive system, and lightweight carbon fiber polymer roof, designed to reduce weight while increasing rigidity. The body is constructed using a combination of carbon fiber and aluminum, making it both strong and light. With a curb weight of just under 1,300 kg, the GR Yaris offers incredible handling, agility, and responsiveness. Short overhangs and a wide track enhance its stability, allowing for exceptional cornering capabilities, while the sophisticated suspension system ensures comfort on both smooth roads and rough rally stages.

"The GR Yaris is the best hot hatch, bar none — it's a car that brings the thrills of rally racing to everyday driving." - Shaswar, 2024

What truly sets the GR Yaris apart from the competition is its racing pedigree. This car was built to meet homologation requirements for WRC, meaning that the same principles used to create rally-winning machines have been applied to this road-going version. The engineers at Toyota have poured their knowledge and experience from the WRC directly into the GR Yaris, resulting in a car that is as at home on a mountain pass as it is on a dirt rally stage.

In terms of aesthetics, the GR Yaris looks as aggressive as it drives. Its wide fenders, flared wheel arches, and lowered stance give it a distinctive rally car appearance. Inside, the cabin combines sportiness with practicality. The driver-focused interior features bolstered sports seats, a leather-wrapped steering wheel, and an intuitive dashboard with a digital display. Despite its racing roots, the GR Yaris is still a practical hatchback, offering modern comforts like climate control, a premium sound system, and advanced driver-assist technologies.

Whether you're an enthusiast who loves pushing a car to its limits or simply someone looking for a daily driver with a bit more excitement, the GR Yaris strikes a perfect balance. Its unique combination of power, agility, and advanced engineering makes it a standout choice in a sea of hot hatches. The car's ability to blend everyday usability with motorsport-inspired performance has garnered praise from both reviewers and drivers alike. If you're looking for a car that can provide rally-level thrills in a daily package, the GR Yaris is, without question, a top contender.
